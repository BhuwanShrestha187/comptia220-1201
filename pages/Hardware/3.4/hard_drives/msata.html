What is mSATA?

Key Characteristics & Compatibility

Advantages (at the time)

  • Very small size ‚Üí fit in thin laptops
  • SATA interface ‚Üí easy upgrade from HDD
  • Used same slot as mPCIe ‚Üí multi-purpose

Disadvantages & Why Replaced by M.2

  • Limited to SATA speeds (no PCIe/NVMe support)
  • Small capacity compared to modern M.2 SSDs
  • No backward/forward compatibility with M.2 slots
  • M.2 offers both SATA and NVMe + smaller/larger options

mSATA Troubleshooting & Replacement Notes

  • No slot available ‚Üí mSATA slots are rare on post-2015 systems
